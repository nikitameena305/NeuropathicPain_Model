Week 1 Morphology Decision

DRG decision:
Primary choice will be based on the NeuroMorpho DRG candidate table if a suitable rat/mouse
dorsal root ganglion neuron is present with soma diameter <30 um, axon present, and
pseudounipolar morphology.

If no suitable DRG morphology passes these criteria:
No suitable cell found -- will use Farooqui ModelDB 2018004 fallback.

SDH decision:
For Week 1 and early circuit benchmarking, SDH will use Medlock ModelDB 267056 point-neuron
template because this is the assigned SDH circuit model and is standard for synaptic input
modelling.

Group 3 focus:
The SDH analysis will emphasize GABAergic inhibitory interneuron/disinhibition logic because
reduced inhibitory control is a major mechanism of neuropathic pain central sensitization.
